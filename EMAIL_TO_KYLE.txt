BIGGEST CHALLENGE:
Query "how many customer complains are still open?" failed due to classifier matching "complaint" (noun) but missing "complains" (verb). Fixed by adding "complain" to CSV indicators to catch all variations. Also learned about Python bytecode cache invalidation in production deployments.
